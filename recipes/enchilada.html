Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Guajillo chile
  • Ancho chile
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas

    Let's start making the sauce.

  1. Start lighting the chiles on a hot griddle, pressing them down with the help of a spatula
    but making sure not to burn them.
  2. Once toasted, place them on pot with water and cook them over low heat for 15 minutes
    or until they are soft.
  3. Let the chiles cool for about 15 minutes and drain them to place them in the blender along
    with the garlic cloves. Add half a cup of water and blend until obtaining a smooth sauce.
    Season with oregano, salt and pepper.

    4. Now let's work with the tortillas

  4. In a large pan add 2 tablespoons of oil and heat over medium heat. Then start frying the
    tortillas (add oil as needed).
  5. Dip the tortillas one by one and from both sides until they are well soaked
  6. Then place the tortilla in the pan with the hot oil and fry both sides (this only takes
    a few seconds) and place the enchilada on a plate.
  7. Fill the enchiladas with cheese to then fold and roll them just as a flauta.
