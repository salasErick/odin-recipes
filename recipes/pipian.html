Pipian con pollo

Pipian is a thick sauce prepared with seeds (usually pumpkin seeds) but you can perfectly
use sesame seeds. In this recipe I'll use shredded chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • 2 pieces of ancho chile
  • 100 grams of pumpkin seeds
  • 1 piece of onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 100 grams of peanuts
  1. Remove the tail and seeds from the chiles and toast them on a griddle.
  2. Boil the tomatoes along the roasted chiles.
  3. Wash the chicken and cook it with water and a piece of onion and garlic.
  4. Separately, toast the pumpkin seeds and peanuts to prepare the pipian.
  5. Blend together the tomato and the chiles
  6. While the tomato sauce is seasoning, grind the pumpkin seeds and the peanuts with some
    broth from the chicken we just cooked and add it to the tomato sauce.
  7. Shred the chicken and add it to the mixture.
