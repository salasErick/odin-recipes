Tacos

Tacos are the most famous mexican dish, here's the way I know to make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • Tortillas
  • 1/2 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 tomato
  1. Cook beef in a pan over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes, sirring occasionally.
  2. Shred half a lettuce and cut the tomato into slices.
  3. Fry a tortilla making a taco shape as it starts to harden.
  4. Fill the taco with ground beef and put lettuce and tomato on top.
