Mexican Cuisine

As with every dish from any culture, there isn't a single way to prepare them. Some people think that any changes made
to the dish they know is a disgrace and there's a single and correct way to prepare it. Meaning that if you don't have
the entire list of ingredients following it to the letter, you'd better not make it. People tend to make variations based
on the ingredients that are available and I believe that being able to improvise while making a dish follows the artistry
behind cooking.

These are all recipes I learned from mom. I hope you like them.
